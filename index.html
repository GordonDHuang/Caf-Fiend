A tracker for your caffeine intake.

Caf-Fiend is your ultimate caffeine companion, seamlessly merging augmented reality (AR), artificial intelligence (AI), and user input to help you manage and monitor your daily caffeine consumption with precision. With Caf-Fiend not only provides real-time tracking but also offers personalized guidance, ensuring that you stay well within recommended daily caffeine limits for a healthier, more balanced lifestyle. Beyond daily tracking, Caf-Fiend lets you log your caffeine intake by day, week, month, and year, granting you invaluable insights into your long-term health and wellness patterns. With its user-friendly interface, Caf-Fiend equips you to navigate your caffeine history effortlessly, empowering you to take charge of your caffeine habits, whether you're a coffee aficionado, a tea lover, or an energy drink enthusiast. In today's fast-paced world, Caf-Fiend stands as your steadfast wellness partner, guiding you toward responsible and sustainable caffeine consumption, so you can lead a healthier and more caffeine-conscious life with confidence.
